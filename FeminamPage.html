Do you dare to go Into the Darkness? Brought to you by Feminam Studios, Into the Darkness is an interactive story game, that takes you on an exciting journey through a kingdom that has been over come by darkness. Yuna a princess who has lost all her memories must regain them all if she is to save herself,her kingdom, and her friends.Will she make it in time before he taints her heart? Help Yuna gain all of her memories, but becareful don't let the Darkness consume you.
